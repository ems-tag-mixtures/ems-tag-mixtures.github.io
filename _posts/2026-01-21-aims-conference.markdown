Dear colleagues,

As you might already know, we (Miroslav Bulíček, Vít Průša and Ondřej Souček) are organising a special session "Mixtures: Modeling, analysis and computing" at the 15th AIMS Conference, Athens, Greece. We have invited (amongst others) many young people working in mixture theory to expand our community. The list of confirmed session speakers is already available at [conference webpage](https://aimsconference.org/AIMS-Conference/conf-reg2026/ss/changeApp12.php?ssid=120). For those of you who were invited to give a talk we recall that the abstract submission deadline is on 31st March 2026. 

If you would like to add a minisymposium, school, or any other type of event to the list of TAG activities, please inform Vit Prusa ([prusv@karlin.mff.cuni.cz](mailto:prusv@karlin.mff.cuni.cz)) and me ([malek@karlin.mff.cuni.cz](mailto:malek@karlin.mff.cuni.cz)). The existing mailing list (thanks to Virginie!) can be used to circulate information about these events and to reach out to potential participants.

Best wishes, 
Vít Průša
